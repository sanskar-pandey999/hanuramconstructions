About Hanuram Construction

Building on a Foundation of Vision and Dedication

Our Story

Hanuram Construction is a budding venture driven by the vision of a passionate founder, SANSKAR PANDEY. While the company is yet to embark on its first official project, the foundation is built upon years of dedicated experience and a deep-seated commitment to the principles of quality construction and client satisfaction.

Meet the Founder: SANSKAR PANDEY

Beginning his career in construction, Sanskar is enthusiastic about revolutionizing the public's perception of civil engineering and its professionals. This initial exposure ignited a deep-seated passion for the tangible impact of buildingâ€”the power to bring blueprints to life as enduring structures.

This passion propelled Sanskar through his schooling at St. Joseph's Convent Senior Secondary School, Sagar, and into his Bachelors and Masters in Civil Engineering at IIT BHU. It was during his fourth year of college that he leveraged this drive, not just for academics, but to launch his own entrepreneurial endeavor.

At its core, the vision for Hanuram Construction is to democratize access to quality civil engineering services, enabling everyone to realize their dream residence with unwavering safety and genuine care. We recognize a significant gap where aspiring homeowners lack affordable, expert guidance, and many qualified civil engineers struggle for practical opportunities. Hanuram Construction bridges this gap, creating a supportive ecosystem where engineers can hone their skills by directly addressing customer needs. Sanskar envisions a construction process built on transparency, active collaboration, and a steadfast commitment to delivering exceptional results that truly surpass client expectations.

Currently, Sanskar is diligently working on establishing Hanuram Construction's core values and operational framework, focusing on building a robust foundation. His commitment is to launch Hanuram Construction as a symbol of trust, quality, and innovation in the construction sector, particularly here in INDIA.
