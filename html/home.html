Renovated Residentials

We offer a unique opportunity for individuals to purchase properties on behalf of our company, earning commissions for successful transactions. Our company representative carefully assesses the neighborhood, market trends, and potential profitability before acquiring any property.

Once listed on our website, the highest bidder secures the sales rights and a commission. Upon acquisition, our interior designer begins work immediately. If we secure a net profit before renovations, buyers will have the option to customize the property according to their vision with the help of our expert team. However, if profits are unsatisfactory, we will renovate the property before putting it up for sale.

Additionally, buyers of these exclusive properties will receive a special membership card, granting them free entry into our society complexes. Their names and photos will be featured on our website, recognizing them as valued members of our community.

Constructed Residentials

We specialize in turning your vision of a dream home into reality, starting from the ground up, with minimal upfront investment. Our unique approach begins with a comprehensive consultation via video calls, where our expert engineers and architects collaborate with you to design a personalized residential building tailored to your pre-owned land. We handle every stage of the process, from budget determination and blueprint creation to obtaining necessary permits, all managed by our experienced site supervisors and civil engineers. We seamlessly integrate essential utilities like plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems, followed by insulation, drywall, and interior finishing, including painting, flooring, and fixture installation. Finally, we handle final inspections, secure your certificate of occupancy, and prepare your home for your move-in, delivering a complete, stress-free construction experience.
